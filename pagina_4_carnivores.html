Dinosaur Dino Info
Tyrannosaurus Rex,
is probably the most famous dinosaur of all time. The T-Rex is dinosaur you can't put with other dinosaurs because he will fight/eat them. The only dinosaurs he can live with are the large herbivores. The T-Rex can be unlocked by traveling to Isla Tacano. When you arive at Isla Tacano you need to build an expidition center and you can do expeditions to locations that have the T-Rex. When the genome is at 50% you can make him.
The Indominus Rex is in the movies a hybrid dinosaur made by mixing the Tyrannosaur Rex and the Velociraptor. To unlock him in game you have to be on Isla Pena and you need the Velociraptor and the T-Rex to be a complete genome (100%) and you need to do a 2 milion dollar research task until you can make him. The nice thing about the Indominus is that he has a high rating and is instantly at 100% genome which means without genetic upgrades he can not fail.
The spinosaurus is the main dinosaur of Jurassic Park III. You unlock the Spinosaurus on Isla Sorna by getting to the island. When you get on the island there is a wild Spino walking around with a couple of herds of stegosauruses. If you don't deal with the Spino on time she will eat some stegosauruses and if you're not careful he might eat some guests as well. The spinosaurus is one of the 2 dinosaurs that eat from a fish feeder.
The Allosaurus is a dinosaur that isn't really seen in the movies until Fallen Kingdom. Allosaurus can be unlockedby doing Science divisions contracts on Isla Sorna. The Allosaurus is found in North America on the expedition map of the expedition center.
The Giganotosaurus is one of the largest species of carnivores and is one of the main dinosaurs in Jurassic World Dominion. The Giganotosaurus is unlocked on the island Isla Pena by completing the Security Division mission. The Giganotosaurus is found in South America on the expedition map of the expedition center.
The Ceratosaurus is the first carnivore you unlock in the game. You get to see the Ceratosaurus in Jurassic Park III, but only for a split second so people might forget that it is in the movies at all. The Ceratosaurus is found in Europa and in North America on the expedition map of the expedition center.
The Metriacanthosaurus might fall under the large carnivores but is quite a bit tinier than the other large dinosaurs. The Metriacanthosaurus should have been in Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom but its scene was cut, it had no other appearances in the movies. The Metriacanthosaurus is unlocked by completing the security mission on Isla Tacano. The Metriacanthosaurus is found in the UK on the expedition map of the expedition center.
The Indoraptor is (if you complete island by island) the last dinosaur you unlock in the game. You unlock the Indoraptor by getting the security reputation almost to the fullest and getting the Indominus Rex and the Velociraptor genome to 100%. The Indoraptor is the main dinosaur villain in Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom. The Indoraptor is not found on the Expedition map because it is a hybird dinosaurs which is created in a lab.
the Baryonyx is together with the Spinosaurus (in the base game) the only dinosaurs who can eat out of a fish feeder. The Baryonyx has an appearance in Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom and has quite a big role in the netflix series Camp Cretaceous. The Baryonyx is unlocked on Isla Muerta by getting the security division reputation bar high enough. The baryonyx is found on the expedition map in the UK of the expedition center.
The Carnotaurus is famous because of its little arms. This Dinosaurs has a very big role in the netflix series Camp Cretaceous. There are a lot of encounters with Toro (the name of the Carnotaurus in the series) and the main characters. The Carnotaurus is unlocked on Isla Pena by getting the science division reputation to the last reward. the Carnotaurus is found in South America on the expedition map of the expedition center.

small carnivores

Dinosaur Dino Info
The Velociraptor is, like the T-Rex, also one of the most famous dinosaurs ever. This dinosaurs has appeard in every Jurassic project so far. The Velociraptor is a very social dinosaur so it needs some friends. If you put a Velociraptor alone in an enclosure she will get mad and attack the fence/guests, so always put more then one Velociraptor in an enclosure to keep them happy. You unlock the Velociraptor by arriving at the second island, Isla Muerta. The Velociraptor is found in Asia on the expedition map of the expedition center.
The Dilophosaurus is a small dinosaur that spits venom at her prey to blind them. She was introduced in the first Jurassic Park and she was the dinosaur that killed Dennis Nedry. The specie wasn't shown again until Jurassic World Dominion where it showed up again to attack the main characters. The Dilophosaurus is unlocked on Isla Muerta and is found in Asia on the expedition map of the expedition center.
The Deinonychus is another small dinosaur in the Jurassic World franchise. It is just like the velociraptor a very social carnivore. You need at least 3 Deinonychus in 1 enclosure to keep it happy. His appearence in the new movie, Jurassic World Dominion, was cut and the only other appearence in the movies is a hologram in Jurassic World. This dinosaur is unlocked on Isla Tacano and is found in North America on the expedition map of the expedition center.
