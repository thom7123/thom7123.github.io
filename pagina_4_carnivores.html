Dinosaur Dino Info
Tyrannosaurus Rex,
is probably the most famous dinosaur of all time. The T-Rex is dinosaur you can't put with other dinosaurs because he will fight/eat them. The only dinosaurs he can live with are the large herbivores. The T-Rex can be unlocked by traveling to Isla Tacano. When you arive at Isla Tacano you need to build an expidition center and you can do expeditions to locations that have the T-Rex. When the genome is at 50% you can make him.
The Indominus Rex is in the movies a hybrid dinosaur made by mixing the Tyrannosaur Rex and the Velociraptor. To unlock him in game you have to be on Isla Pena and you need the Velociraptor and the T-Rex to be a complete genome (100%) and you need to do a 2 milion dollar research task until you can make him. The nice thing about the Indominus is that he has a high rating and is instantly at 100% genome which means without genetic upgrades he can not fail.
The spinosaurus is the main dinosaur of Jurassic Park III. You unlock the Spinosaurus on Isla Sorna by getting to the island. When you get on the island there is a wild Spino walking around with a couple of herds of stegosauruses. If you don't deal with the Spino on time she will eat some stegosauruses and if you're not careful he might eat some guests as well. The spinosaurus is one of the 2 dinosaurs that eat from a fish feeder.
The Allosaurus is a dinosaur that isn't really seen in the movies until Fallen Kingdom. Allosaurus can be unlockedby doing Science divisions contracts on Isla Sorna. The Allosaurus is found in North America on the expedition map of the expedition center.
The Giganotosaurus is one of the largest species of carnivores and is one of the main dinosaurs in Jurassic World Dominion. The Giganotosaurus is unlocked on the island Isla Pena by completing the Security Division mission. The Giganotosaurus is found in South America on the expedition map of the expedition center.

Dinosaur Dino Info
The Velociraptor is, like the T-Rex, also one of the most famous dinosaurs ever. This dinosaurs has appeard in every Jurassic project so far. The Velociraptor is a very social dinosaur so it needs some friends. If you put a Velociraptor alone in an enclosure she will get mad and attack the fence/guests, so always put more then one Velociraptor in an enclosure to keep them happy. You unlock the Velociraptor by arriving at the second island, Isla Muerta. The Velociraptor is found in Asia on the expedition map of the expedition center.
The Dilophosaurus is a small dinosaur that spits venom at her prey. She was introduced in the first Jurassic Park and she was the dinosaur that killed Dennis Nedry. The specie wasn't shown again until Jurassic World Dominion where it showed up again to attack the main characters. The Dilophosaurus is unlocked on Isla Muerta and is found in Asia on the expedition map of the expedition center.
