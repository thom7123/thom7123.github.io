Isla Matanceros

Isla Matanceros is the first island of 'The Five Deaths' you
get to build your park on
and they are called 'The Five Deaths because people went to
these islands and never came back (according to the movies that is).
You start with the indroduction of how to start
and how to keep an island going without going broke.

Isla Muerta

Isla Muerta is the second island of the base game.
You start the island with a half park that was broken down by a strom
and have to rebuilt it and make it back in to a good running park.

Isla Tacano

After Isla Muerta you get to go Isla Tacano.
On this island you start with what look like the remains of
Jurassic World and you are very much in debt
and have to sell a couple buildings to get out of the debt.
Afer that you have to do what you have to
do in every other island.

Isla Pena

The fourth island is called Isla Pena and is the smallest island by far
and that makes it a pretty hard island to beat.
On the isalnd it looks like it's always night and that makes the island look
extra ominous besides all the dinosaur roars in the background and the introduction of the tornado during stroms.

Isla Sorna

Isla Sorna is the island where Jurasic Park II and Jurassic park III took place
and the last island of the stroy mode.
On this island you begin like on Isla Tacano,
only there are some wild stegosaurus and one wild Spinosaurus you have to deal with.
On this island you have to use everything you have learnt
in the past islands to complete this island.

Isla Nublar

Isla Nublar is the island where the original Jurassic Park and Jurassic World were build,
which makes it one of the most popular island in the game.
This island is a Sandbox island which means that you have unlimited money
and Isla Nublar offers a large space to build,
in other words you can do whatever you want. You can build dream dinosaur park,
or you can make dinosaurs fight in a giant arena.
